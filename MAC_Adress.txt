Mac Address is the physical address of a device.
It is 48 bits long. The bits are divide with 6 group. Each group has 2 hexadecimal numbers, each hexadecimal number consist of
4 bits. because 2 ^ 4 = 16(hexadecimal).

So, 
=>  4(bits) * 2 = 8(octal)
=>  8 * 6(group) = 48(total bits)
